Easy Gyudon Recipe!!

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

  • thinly sliced beef
  • cooked rice
  • Scallions
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • KBBQ marinade
  • Egg yolk (optional)

Step 2:

Mix the soy sauce, sugar, and KBBQ marinade with the beef. There is no need to marinate.

Step 3:

Heat pan to medium-high heat and put oil and scallions on it

Step 5:

Fry the beef with until cooked

Step 6:

Place the beef on the bowl of rice and add an egg yolk on top

Step 7:

Enjoy!
